Guidelines

Here you can find the guidelines for the Digital Analysis and Algorithm subject. Make sure to follow these guidelines to succeed in this course..
  1. Attend all lectures and participate in discussions.
  2. Complete all assignments and projects on time.
  3. Read the recommended textbooks and articles.
  4. Practice problems regularly to improve your understanding.
  5. Form study groups to discuss and solve problems collaboratively.
  6. Seek help from the instructor or teaching assistants when needed.
  7. Stay updated with any announcements and changes in the schedule.
